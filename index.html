Welcome to Gulf Shores Flying Club

Gulf Shores Flying Club is located at Gulf Shores International Airport in Gulf Shores, AL (KJKA). We are now accepting membership applications. Whether you're an experienced pilot, a student pilot embarking on your aviation journey, or simply captivated by the world of flight, we invite you to join our club! As a member, you'll have access to our Cessna 152. For further details and to begin your membership process, please reach out to us at 262-573-5219 and check out our join page. Fly with us and experience the thrill of aviation at Gulf Shores Flying Club.

Why join a flying club?

  • Accessibility

    • While flight schools excel at training new pilots, the accessibility of aircraft can become challenging once you obtain your license. Renting an airplane often comes with limitations, as availability is primarily reserved for students undergoing training. However, joining a flying club eliminates this hassle. By simply scheduling your flight, the airplane becomes readily available for you to enjoy. Experience the freedom of having easy access to aircraft as a member of our flying club, ensuring you can fully embrace your passion for flying without unnecessary obstacles.

  • Affordability

    • Joining our flying club provides an affordable way to experience the joys of aviation. As a member, you can enjoy access to our well-maintained Cessna 152 at a fraction of the cost of individual ownership or renting. By sharing expenses among our club members, we make flying more accessible and budget-friendly. Take to the skies, enhance your skills, and connect with fellow aviation enthusiasts, all while keeping your expenses in check.
